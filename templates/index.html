Health-Hive??

Health-Hive is a BLOCKCHAIN based extended Electronic Health Record (EHR) that facilitates intuitive formatting and enhanced interaction of health records that can be transferred among health centers. We aim at placing the patient in control of their medical data, giving them the power to share the single most,comprehensive version of their record.
