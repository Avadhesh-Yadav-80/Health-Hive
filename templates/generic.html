KNOW MORE ABOUT US

Health-Hive as the name goes is an amalgum of PATIENT AND RECORD.

Our aim is to put the patient in control of their medical data, giving them the power to share the single, most comprehensive version of their record, with every organisation in their medical network. Fragmented, soiled patient records create inefficiencies and inaccuracies across the breadth of the healthcare system. Our uses blockchain technology to securely manage health records for a collaborative, smart approach to healthcare.

Adopting blockchain into the medical industry would convert all the medical data into one anonymous database where the patient holds the power. With this implementation, the problem of interoperability, that is, lack of compatibility of EHR in different healthcare entities will be solved. Insurance providers can make sure the data is not falsified as the blockchain is immutable. Security and privacy is also achieved making it patient-centered.
